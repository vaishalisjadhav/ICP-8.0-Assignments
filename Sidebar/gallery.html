Coldy Bites

Welcome to our Gallery

Butter Pecan

Butter Pecan ice cream combines buttery, rich flavor with crunchy, roasted pecans. This creamy, nutty delight is perfect for a satisfying and indulgent treat.

Chocolate Custard

Chocolate Custard ice cream is a rich and creamy treat made with real egg yolks for an extra smooth texture. Its deep chocolate flavor offers a luxurious and indulgent experience in every bite.

Chocolate Fudge Brownie

Chocolate Fudge Brownie ice cream is a decadent delight, blending rich chocolate ice cream with chunks of fudgy brownie pieces. Indulge in the creamy texture and intense chocolate flavor, heightened by the irresistible bites of gooey brownie chunks. It's the ultimate treat for chocolate lovers craving a perfect balance of sweetness and indulgence.

Mango Sorbet

Mango Sorbet is a refreshing and tropical delight, bursting with the vibrant flavor of ripe mangoes. This dairy-free frozen dessert offers a smooth and velvety texture, perfect for cooling off on a hot day. Indulge in the sweet and tangy taste of pure mango goodness with every spoonful.

Mint Chocolate Chip

Mint Chocolate Chip ice cream is a refreshing twist on a classic favorite, featuring creamy mint ice cream infused with crunchy chocolate chips. Indulge in the cool, minty flavor that perfectly complements the rich bursts of chocolate in every bite. It's a delightful treat that's both refreshing and indulgent, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth cravings.

Thai Rolled Ice Cream

Thai Rolled Ice Cream is a delightful frozen treat originating from Thailand, made by pouring a liquid ice cream base onto a cold metal surface where it's mixed with various toppings and flavors, then skillfully rolled into thin scrolls. This unique dessert offers a combination of creamy texture and customizable flavors, making it both a delicious and entertaining experience. Enjoy watching your ice cream being freshly prepared and savor the delicious taste of each handcrafted roll.
